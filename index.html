Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the team size?

    Two. One if you're brave or like challenges.

  • Who can participate?

    Everyone is welcome to apply, students, professionals, or sentient ChatGPT wrappers. Parents consent is required for applicants under 18.

  • Do I need an idea to apply ?

    You should have some idea of what you're going to be doing, but if you don't, submit a really good application.

  • How does the application process work?

    Go to devfolio and register. Write about what you plan on doing and why you want to participate. We want people who can think differently and are inspired by the weirdest things. Mostly, surprise us! (and don't be boring)

  • When will applications close?

    TBD

  • Why should I participate?

    Besides the schwag, free food, unlimited coffee and lots of prizes? Meeting people who you'll most likely click with, I suppose. But I'll leave that up to you.
